About Us

Coffee Cafe is your neighborhood coffee bar located in the heart of Bucktown, Chicago. We pride ourselves on serving high-quality coffee sourced from sustainable farms around the world.

Our cozy ambiance and friendly staff create the perfect environment for enjoying your favorite brew or catching up with friends. Whether you're seeking a quiet spot to work or a lively atmosphere for socializing, Coffee Cafe has something for everyone.
